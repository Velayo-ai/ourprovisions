The three states

  • Large — today's header. Wordmark is the hero.
  • Small — demoted to a mark. Photo gets the air.
  • Hidden — the middle band is the photo, nothing else. The household name stays in the top bar where it always was.

Why the control disappears with no photo

Your rule, and it's the right one. The setting appears at the moment it means something — you added a photo, now there's a reason to care how loud the brand is over it. No photo means no empty-band case to design around.

Dormancy

Set Hidden, then someone removes the photo: the wordmark returns to hero and the setting sits dormant, not cleared. Add a photo again and it reapplies. Don't "fix" this later by resetting on removal.

Open

Safe-area: the scrim's darkest stop is at 0% of the header, not of the screen — may collide with the Dynamic Island. Needs a device check.
